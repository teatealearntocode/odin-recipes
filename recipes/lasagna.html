This is the recipe of lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Parmesan
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water for 12 min
  2. Drain the water, put cooking oil into the pan
  3. Stir-fry the pasta, add tomato sauce
  4. Bake for 20 min
  5. Add parmesan, bake for another 20 min
  6. Enjoy
